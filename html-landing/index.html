Our Services

Discover a Secure Cloud IT Space

  • FinOps Services

    Our approach to cloud cost management integrates the FinOps framework, leveraging industry-proven practices recommended by cloud providers. Through iterative phases—Inform, Optimize, and Operate we implement multiple FinOps domains to optimize cloud usage, quantify business value, and manage costs effectively.

  • Cloud Migration

    Our Cloud migration as a service starts with assessment and planning, evaluating current systems for cloud readiness. Migration strategies like rehosting, re-platforming, or refactoring are then implemented, followed by execution, testing, validation, and optimization to ensure a seamless transition and operational efficiency in the cloud.

  • Architecture Services

    Our Architecture service offers design solutions for business applications and workloads destined for public cloud platforms. It includes various frameworks like Layered, Client-server, Event-driven, Plug-in, Microservices, and Cloud-native architectures, each tailored to specific industry needs for scalability, efficiency, and deployment flexibility.

  • Cloud Consulting Services

    Our consultants leverage deep industry expertise to help businesses across healthcare, education, finance, and e-commerce sectors formulate and implement tailored cloud strategies. Services span cloud strategy, migration, security, compliance, disaster recovery, serverless solutions, and operational governance, ensuring optimized cloud adoption aligned with business objectives.
